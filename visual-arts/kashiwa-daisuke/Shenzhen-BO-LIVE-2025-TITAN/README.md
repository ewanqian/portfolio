# 深圳 BO LIVE 2025 — Visual Art / Audiovisual Performance

**Project Title**  
**Mechanical Photosynthesis: TITAN’s Holographic Sound Forest** (机械光合：TITAN的全息声林)  
*KASHIWA Daisuke x Yuki Murata x Ewan Qian Audiovisual Performance Special*

**Date**  
October 21, 2025

**Location**  
BO LIVE, Shenzhen, China

**Artists**  
- KASHIWA Daisuke  
- Yuki Murata  
- Ewan Qian

---

## Project Notes
这场特别视听表演围绕《TITAN》的氛围与声音世界展开 (This special audiovisual performance was developed around the atmosphere and sonic world of *TITAN*).  
视觉语言聚焦于雾气、光线、白空间深度、环境过渡、沉浸错觉与感知运动 (The visual language focused on fog, light, white spatial depth, ambient transitions, immersive illusion, and perceptual movement).  
部分段落由强烈的音频交互结构驱动 (Some sections were driven by strong audio-reactive structures)，而另一些则追求更柔和、漂浮、治愈且沉思的状态 (while others aimed for a softer, floating, healing, and more contemplative state).
